ELECTRONIC INVOICING

We make your life easier and help conserve our natural resources with electronic invoices, receipts, and payment. As a Lehigh customer, you'll automatically have it all continuously at your fingertips through Electronic Invoice Presentment & Payment (EIPP).

So what does all this mean to you? Enjoy no more lost invoices, review your invoices online, no more waiting for the mail, get account access 24/7/365, invoice notices emailed to you daily (which can be sent to more than one person), pay online by credit card or echeck, receive payment verification, and less keying required by your employees.
